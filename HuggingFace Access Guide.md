Hugging Face Access Guide - Step by Step

Quick Start: 3-Step Process

Step 1: Create Account
1. Go to [huggingface.co](https://huggingface.co/)
2. Click "Sign Up"
3. Use your email address
4. Verify email address

Step 2: Request LLAMA-3.1 Access
1. Go to: [Meta-Llama-3.1-8B-Instruct](https://huggingface.co/meta-llama/Meta-Llama-3.1-8B-Instruct)
2. Click the "Request access" button (blue button)
3. Fill out the form:
   - Organization: [Your University/Company]
   - Use case: PhD research on cybersecurity threat hunting using domain-specific fine-tuning"
   - Intended users: "Academic research and personal use"
   - Additional information: "Researching domain adaptation of LLAMA-3.1 for MITRE ATT&CK framework"
4. Submit the request

Step 3: Get Access Token
Wait for approval email (usually 1-2 hours), then:
1. Go to [Settings > Access Tokens](https://huggingface.co/settings/tokens)
2. Click "New token"
3. Name: "Colab-Training-Research"
4. Select: "Read" and "Write" permissions
5. Click "Generate token"
6. Copy the token (starts with `hf_`)

## Tips for Approval

### What to Expect:
- Automatic approval for most academic users
- Manual review for non-academic users (may take longer)
- Email notification when access is granted

### If Request is Rejected:
1. Check your email for specific reasons
2. Try again with more detailed use case
3. Contact Meta directly if needed
